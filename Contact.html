How To Reach Us

Ramanujan House is located inside the TIFR Housing Colony on Homi Bhabha Road, opposite the TIFR Campus.


You can take a taxi and ask the driver to take you to the Navy Nagar Bus Terminus in Colaba.


You may also use:

  • Bus No. 3 from CST
  • Bus No. 11 from CST
  • Bus No. 125 from CST
  • Bus No. 137 from Churchgate Station

Get down at the last stop and proceed towards the end of the road where the TIFR Campus is located.
